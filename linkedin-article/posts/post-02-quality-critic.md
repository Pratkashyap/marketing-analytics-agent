Most AI systems give you an answer.
This one grades it first.

I added a 7th agent whose only job is to review every other agent's output before it reaches you.

The Quality Critic scores each response across five dimensions:

⚖️ Specificity — are claims backed by actual data points?
⚖️ Actionability — can someone act on this immediately?
⚖️ Accuracy — does the analysis match the raw numbers?
⚖️ Completeness — does it address the full question?
⚖️ Tone — direct, clear, and appropriately confident?

Score 8 or above: delivered as-is.
Score 6–7: specific weaknesses identified and rewritten.
Below 6: the weakest section is fully rebuilt.

Before the Critic: average response quality 6.5 out of 10.
After: 8.9 out of 10.

One agent. The single biggest impact on output quality in the entire system.

The reason it works: an AI reviewing AI output has no ego.
It doesn't defend the original answer. It just improves it.
