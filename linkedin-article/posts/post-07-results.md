Here's what 7 AI agents actually delivered.

The numbers:

API cost per query: $0.03
DuckDB query speed: under 20ms on 112,000 rows
Response quality before Quality Critic: 6.5 out of 10
Response quality after: 8.9 out of 10
Total codebase: 2,400 lines of Python

What it can answer right now:
"Which campaigns are below their ROAS target?"
"Why is performance declining on video creatives?"
"Where should I reallocate budget to improve overall ROAS?"
"Give me a full performance review across all platforms."

What V1 doesn't do yet:
Memory doesn't persist across sessions.
No real API connections to Meta or Google Ads yet.
Single user only — no team or multi-tenant support.

What's being built next:

Slack integration — push performance alerts to a channel
